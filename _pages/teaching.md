## Personal Assessment and Development
At London Business School, I teach Personal Assessment and Development in the Masters in Finance programme each year. There are typically around 170 students from 50 countries with an average of five years' work experience. The course consists of 10 hours intensive learning that blends lectures, case discussions and a group simulation exercise. We cover a range of topics, including cross-cultural leadership, influence, and personality traits.

I received a rating of 4.73/5 (based on 62 student surveys) in the 2025/26 academic year. Selected student comments:
- "William's lectures were very engaging and insightful."
- "William made the sessions very dynamic and interactive, and his approachable personality created a great learning environment."
- "I was impressed with William's ability to engage members of the group with different backgrounds, experiences and confidence levels. This was reflected in the group discussions evolving and becoming more sophisticated, and more members of the cohort contributing."
